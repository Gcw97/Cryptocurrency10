The Future of Cryptocurrency

2020 was a very eccentric year for crytocurrency. The pandemic opened the worlds eyes as to how easily are day to day routine could drastically change in an instance. This opened up conceptual understand and technical curiosity to these blockchains and the endless possibilities they can achieve. Mainstream businesses are starting to adapt the blockchain to their model structure and more supporters start to pour in. However that does not remove the uncertainty behind the blockchain and the place it will take in our world's economic structure. Come 2021 this technology will begin to stretch beyond financial services and start to attract sectors such as Healthcare, transportation, logistics and many more. Supporters see cryptocurreny such as Bitcoin as the future and there is an uproar to purchases them before they come more and more valuable. Although the future may seem bright for Cryptocurrency it is always risky to predict the future.
